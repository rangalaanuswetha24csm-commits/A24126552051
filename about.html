About the Training & Placement Department

Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology & Sciences (ANITS) has a dedicated Training & Placement Department headed by experienced faculty. The department focuses on preparing students for campus recruitment through training, technical skill development, communication skills and personality development.

Students receive aptitude training, coding practice, group discussion sessions, mock interviews and career guidance to improve employability.

Our Department Focuses On

  • Providing campus recruitment training.
  • Technical and communication skill development.
  • Industrial visits and internships.
  • Guest lectures by industry experts.
  • Career guidance and placement assistance.
  • Building industry-institute relationships.
